# HomeFix - Home Services Website

A modern, professional, and responsive website for a home services company operating in Sargodha and Islamabad, Pakistan.

## 🎯 Business Overview

HomeFix provides fast, reliable, and affordable home installation and repair services with a skilled technical team.

## 🧰 Services Offered

- **CCTV Camera Installation & Repair** - Professional security camera installation and maintenance
- **AC Service & Installation** - Complete air conditioning solutions for your comfort
- **Electrical Work (All types)** - All types of electrical installation and repairs
- **Plumbing Services** - Expert plumbing solutions for your home
- **WiFi Installation & Networking** - Fast and reliable internet connectivity setup
- **Home Repair & Maintenance** - Complete home maintenance and repair services

## 🎨 Features

- **Responsive Design** - Mobile-first, works perfectly on all devices
